Privacy Policy of Agartha

Scope of the Policy

This Privacy Policy governs personal information processed by Agito Lab Limited through its websites, mobile applications, games, and other services that link to it (collectively, the “Services”). It clarifies that Agito Lab Limited does not control blockchain protocols where NFTs and other tokens are traded, and thus cannot govern what happens to data on the public blockchain.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Agito Lab Limited may revise the policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated as required by law. Continuing to use the Services after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

Personal Information Collected

Categories of Information:

1.Information Provided Directly:

  • Account Creation: Name, phone number, email address, time zone, and shipping address if you buy merchandise.
  • Purchases and Transactions: Payment details, public wallet addresses (no private keys), shipping and billing information.
  • Identity Verification: Agito Lab Limited may work with third-party vendors to verify identity, which may involve government IDs, Social Security numbers, biometric scans, and other sensitive details. Agito Lab Limited itself does not store biometric data—only the verification result.
  • Communications: Contact details for newsletters, support requests, and feedback submitted via forms.
  • Interactive Features and User Content: Any information you post on publicly accessible platforms, like chat rooms, Discord communities, or social media pages becomes public.
  • Surveys, Sweepstakes, Events: If you participate, you may submit additional personal details.
  • Business Development: Information collected during conferences, trade shows, or other professional engagements.

2.Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Data: Browser/device info, approximate location via IP, browsing activity on the Services, and how you interact with content.
  • Game Usage Information: If you play KOF Agartha-related games, in-game actions and wallet addresses may be tracked.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Agito Lab Limited and third parties may use cookies, local storage, and analytics tools to understand usage patterns and improve services.

3.Information from Third Parties:

Agito Lab Limited may receive data from third-party login services (e.g., social media), partners, or other users who share information about you.

How Agito Lab Limited Uses Your Information

Uses of personal information generally include:

  • To Provide Services (Contractual Necessity): Managing accounts, enabling site features, facilitating purchases, responding to inquiries, and notifying you about changes.
  • Administrative and Legitimate Interest Purposes: Fraud prevention, security, debugging, auditing compliance, analyzing usage, improving services, marketing communication (when permitted), enforcing policies, and conducting research.
  • Marketing and Communications: Sending you promotional messages, event updates, newsletters, and marketing materials. You can opt out of certain marketing activities.
  • With Your Consent: Any purposes for which you explicitly give consent at the time of data collection.

  • On the Blockchain: Blockchain transactions are public. Wallet addresses and associated transaction data are visible and cannot be deleted once recorded. Others may link this data to your identity.
  • With Other Users: Certain profile details or transaction data may be visible to other users of the Services.
  • Third-Party Websites and Applications: If you connect your wallet or interact with third-party apps, those apps’ privacy policies apply. Agito Lab Limited is not responsible for these parties’ data practices.
  • Service Providers and Business Partners: Agito Lab Limited works with identity verification vendors, payment processors, and other service providers who process data on its behalf. Also, it may share information with business partners involved in providing requested products or services.
  • Corporate Affiliates: Personal information may be shared within Agito Lab Limited’s corporate family.
  • For Compliance and Protection: If required by law or necessary for protecting rights and property, Agito Lab Limited may disclose personal data.
  • Business Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, personal information may be transferred.
